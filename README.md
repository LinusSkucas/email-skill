Have Mycroft check your email

## About 
With this skill, Mycroft can check your email. When you ask him, he can list your new emails. He will say the sender and the subject of each email.

## Examples 
* "Check my email"
* "Do I have any email?"
* "Do I have any new emails?"
* "Check my mail"
